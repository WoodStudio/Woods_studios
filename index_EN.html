How we work

Design

At WoodStudio, we specialize in crafting custom-made furniture, meticulously designed and visualized using SketchUp. We offer our clients the opportunity to preview their furniture designs, providing a glimpse into the reality of their envisioned space. Our process ensures transparency and collaboration, allowing clients to make informed decisions before production begins. With our detailed renderings, customers can confidently anticipate the final outcome of their bespoke furniture pieces. Experience the innovation and precision of custom furniture design with WoodStudio.

manufacture

At WoodStudio, we prioritize using the highest quality wood to manufacture our furniture, ensuring durability and beauty in every piece. Our skilled craftsmen utilize a blend of traditional and contemporary machinery to bring your desired furniture design to fruition. By combining time-honored techniques with modern technology, we achieve precision and excellence in every aspect of production. Whether it's hand-carved details or precision cuts, our commitment to craftsmanship shines through in each bespoke creation. Trust WoodStudio to deliver furniture that not only meets but exceeds your expectations for quality and design.

Install

At WoodStudio, we take pride in our careful and professional furniture installation services. Our team goes above and beyond to ensure that each piece is installed with precision and attention to detail. We prioritize understanding the space where the furniture will be placed, ensuring a seamless integration into its surroundings. From measuring to mounting, our meticulous approach guarantees a flawless installation experience for our clients. Trust WoodStudio to transform your space with expertly installed custom furniture.
